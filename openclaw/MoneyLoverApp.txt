Open app "Money Lover"
Navigate to tab "Account"
Tap on "Export to Google sheet" button
In popup "Export to Google sheet", tap on "Wallet" selection to open "Select Wallet" popup
In "Select Wallet" popup, tap on "楽天カード" selection
Wait until back to popup "Export to Google sheet"
In "Time range" selection, tap on "This month"
In "Date" popup, tap on "Between", wait until "Custom" popup is displayed
Tap on "Start date", click "Back" button to select 1st of previous month, and tap on it
Tap on "End date", select current date and tap on it
Tap on "Done" button
Wait until back to popup "Export to Google sheet"
Tap on "Export" button
Wait until "Export successfully" popup is displayed
Tap on "Open" button
